About Havoc Software

Real-World Open Source Solutions

Havoc Software was founded in December of 2023 with the goal of providing real-world solutions through open-source technology. Since its inception, the project has come a long way, and "Copy That" has rapidly evolved into a highly promising tool.

Over the next few months, the project will enter its alpha and beta stages, where the software will be rigorously tested and refined. As an open-source initiative, we invite developers, power users, and enthusiasts to freely contribute to the development and improvement of "Copy That."

Together, we can create tools that are not only innovative but also tailored to meet the needs of users around the world.
